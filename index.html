Last Updated: May 13, 2026

Welcome to Mussa Art Studio ("we," "our," or "us"). We provide professional-grade AI art generation tools. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle data to ensure your creative experience is private and secure.

1. AI GENERATION & PRIVACY

Your creativity is your own. We handle AI generation data with strict privacy protocols:

  • Local Processing: Prompts and configurations are processed to generate artwork. We do NOT store your creative prompts on our permanent servers beyond the immediate generation request.
  • Image Ownership: The images you generate are yours to save and share. We do not claim ownership or use your generated artwork for promotional purposes without your explicit consent.
  • No Training: We do NOT use your private generated images or specific custom prompts to train our global AI models.

2. AI SERVICE TERMS

By using our AI generation features, you agree to the following terms:

  • Content Responsibility: You are solely responsible for the prompts you provide. You must not use the AI to generate illegal, harmful, threatening, abusive, harassing, defamatory, or otherwise objectionable content.
  • Generated Content Ownership: Subject to your compliance with these terms, you own the content you generate using Mussa. However, due to the nature of machine learning, generated content may not be unique and other users may generate similar results.
  • No Infringement: You agree not to use the service to generate content that infringes upon the intellectual property rights of others.
  • Service Limitations: AI-generated content is provided "as is." While we strive for high-quality results, we do not guarantee the accuracy, quality, or suitability of the generated output for any specific purpose.

3. DATA COLLECTION POLICY

Mussa is designed for anonymity and speed. We do NOT collect personally identifiable information (PII).

  • No Accounts: No email, name, or phone number registration is required to use the app.
  • Local Storage: Your history, unlocked styles (Auras), and credits are stored locally on your device or via secure iCloud backup (if enabled by you).
  • Anonymous Analytics: We may collect anonymous usage statistics (e.g., app crashes, feature usage frequency) to improve app performance, but this data cannot be linked to you.

4. IN-APP TRANSACTIONS

Mussa uses Apple's secure App Store infrastructure for all credit purchases.

  • Payment info is handled exclusively by Apple Inc.
  • We never see or store your credit card numbers or banking details.

5. DEVICE PERMISSIONS

  • Photo Library: Required only when you choose to save your generated AI artwork to your device.
  • Haptics & Audio: Used to provide an immersive "Studio Ambience" and tactile feedback during the generation process.
