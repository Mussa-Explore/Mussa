Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 10, 2026

Welcome to Mussa ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and providing a secure, account-free experience. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use the Mussa mobile application.

1. NO PERSONAL DATA COLLECTION

Mussa is designed to respect your anonymity. We do NOT collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII).

  • No registration is required.
  • We do not collect your name, email, phone number, or physical address.
  • We do not access your contacts or messages.

2. DATA STORAGE AND PERSISTENCE

All your app data—including unlocked items, crystal shards, and chat history—is stored locally on your device using standard iOS persistence methods.

  • We do not maintain any cloud-based servers that store your individual usage data.
  • If you delete the app, your locally stored data will be removed.

3. IN-APP PURCHASES

Mussa uses Apple's StoreKit for all financial transactions. All payment processing is handled securely by Apple Inc.

  • We do not have access to your credit card information or billing details.
  • Purchase records are managed by Apple to ensure your transaction security.

4. MEDIA AND PERMISSIONS

  • Save to Gallery: If you choose to save a Mussa artwork, we request "Add to Photos" permission only to write that specific image.
  • Camera: The "Matcher" feature may use camera metadata for simulations, but no images are uploaded to any server.

5. AI CHAT AND INTERACTION

All interactions with the AI Muses are processed within the app's local logic or secure API endpoints. Your chat history remains exclusively on your device.
